Sign Up To Watch Puffer

Puffer is a research project run by Keith Winstein's group at Stanford University. The goal of this project is to investigate methods for improving live streaming of video over the Internet. To that end, we provide live rebroadcasts over the Internet of broadcast television channels available over-the-air in the Bay Area. To learn more about Puffer, please visit our FAQ.

To avoid overwhelming our infrastructure, we are slowing growing the number of users on Puffer, with an eventual goal of 500 simultaneous viewers. As such, signing up for Puffer requires an invitation token. To request an invitation token from the Puffer team, please provide your email below, and we will provide you with one as soon as we can.
